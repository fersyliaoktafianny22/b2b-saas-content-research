This file lists the 10 experts selected for this research project, along with their newsletter links, subscriber counts (where available), and notes on why each was chosen.

Selection criteria: each person actively writes/operates a newsletter focused on B2B SaaS marketing or growth (not just a one-off blog post), has real operating experience (founder, CMO, VP Marketing, or equivalent), and is recommended/cited by other credible people in the space — not just appearing on generic "best newsletter" listicles.
## 1. Kevin Indig — Growth Memo
- Link: https://www.growth-memo.com/about
- 61,573 followers on LinkedIn
- Date Research : 18 July 2026
- Why chosen: Former SEO/Growth lead at Shopify, G2, and Atlassian. Growth Memo focuses on organic growth strategy, SEO, and AI visibility, with deep data-driven case studies rather than generic listicle content. Frequently cited by other respected marketers (Tim Soulo, Cyrus Shepard) as one of the best in the space.
