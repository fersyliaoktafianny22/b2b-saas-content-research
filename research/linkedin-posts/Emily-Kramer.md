## Post LinkedIn 1
**Title :** Demo Request Conversion Optimization

**Link :** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/emilykramer_revenueheropartner-activity-7470886898958315520-wQim?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAETE9j4BLR05wW6Rp6usGnUWg-kWo5O5mFE

**Date :** 13 June 2026

**Summary :** Emily shared research on 100 high-growth B2B companies and found that only 39% allow prospects to book a meeting immediately after submitting a demo request. 
The remaining companies require prospects to wait for follow-up communication, introducing friction into the buying process. She argues that immediate meeting scheduling significantly improves conversion rates and that many SaaS companies are 
unnecessarily losing qualified leads by relying on outdated qualification and routing processes.

**Key Takeaway :** Reducing friction in the demo booking process can substantially improve conversion rates. Companies should prioritize immediate meeting scheduling whenever possible rather than forcing prospects to wait for manual follow-up.
