## Post LinkedIn
**Title :** A Marketing Team's Most Valuable Asset in the AI Era

**Link :** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/kieranjflanagan_a-marketing-teams-most-valuable-asset-for-share-7473367020101791744-zmGQ/?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAETE9j4BLR05wW6Rp6usGnUWg-kWo5O5mFE

**Date :** 19 June 2026

**Summary :** Kieran argues that a marketing team's most valuable asset is no longer content, channels, or tools, but proprietary knowledge. 
As AI makes content creation and execution increasingly accessible, competitive advantage shifts toward unique customer insights, institutional knowledge, and internally developed expertise.
He explains that organizations should focus on capturing, organizing, and operationalizing their knowledge so it can be leveraged by both humans and AI systems. 
Companies that successfully build knowledge assets will be able to create more differentiated marketing strategies and maintain an advantage as AI capabilities become widely available.

**Key Takeaway :** In an AI-driven world, proprietary knowledge becomes the primary competitive advantage. Marketing teams should invest in capturing and structuring unique insights that AI tools can amplify but competitors cannot easily replicate.
