## Post LinkedIn
**Title :** Building an AI-Powered GTM System

**Link :** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/kyle-poyar_a-4-step-guide-to-build-your-ai-gtm-system-share-7473001160740802560-TxUh/?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAETE9j4BLR05wW6Rp6usGnUWg-kWo5O5mFE

**Date :** 18 June 2026

**Summary :** Kyle introduces a four-step framework for building an AI-powered go-to-market (GTM) system. He argues that AI should not be treated as a collection of isolated tools but as an integrated operating system that supports the entire customer journey.
The framework emphasizes connecting customer data, defining repeatable workflows, deploying AI agents for execution, and continuously measuring performance. 
Rather than replacing teams, AI should be used to increase efficiency, improve decision-making, and scale go-to-market activities.

**Key Takeaway :** Companies gain the most value from AI when they build connected GTM systems that integrate data, workflows, and execution instead of adopting disconnected AI tools.
