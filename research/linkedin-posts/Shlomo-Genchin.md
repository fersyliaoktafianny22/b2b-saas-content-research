## Post LinkedIn
**Title :** Using AI Storyboards to Sell Video Ad Concepts

**Link :** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/shlomogenchin_without-a-good-storyboard-selling-a-video-activity-7469673222578552832-fq4P?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAETE9j4BLR05wW6Rp6usGnUWg-kWo5O5mFE

**Date :** 13 June 2026

**Summary :** Shlomo explains that selling a video advertisement concept without a storyboard is extremely difficult because clients need to visualize the final outcome before approving production. He demonstrates a workflow using Figma Weave and AI image generation to rapidly create storyboards from a reference character and a predefined shot list.
His process involves defining camera angles and scenes, generating multiple visual variations, and assembling them into a storyboard within minutes. 
This allows marketers and creative teams to communicate ideas more effectively before investing in full video production.

**Key Takeaway :** AI-powered storyboarding can significantly reduce the time required to pitch and validate video advertising concepts while improving communication between creative teams and clients.
