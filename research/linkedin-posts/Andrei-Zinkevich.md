## Post LinkedIn
**Title :** Strategic Accounts Require Multi-Threaded Engagement

**Link :** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/azinkevich_nobody-from-my-strategic-accounts-literally-share-7472977220005773312-UuzM/?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAETE9j4BLR05wW6Rp6usGnUWg-kWo5O5mFE

**Date :** 18 June 2026

**Summary :** Andrei highlights a common challenge in B2B sales and marketing: organizations often focus their efforts on a single contact within a target account and become frustrated when engagement stalls. 
He argues that relying on one champion is risky because buying decisions are typically influenced by multiple stakeholders across departments.
The post emphasizes the importance of building relationships with several decision-makers and influencers within strategic accounts. 
By creating multiple touchpoints and maintaining engagement across different stakeholders, companies can reduce risk and improve their chances of advancing opportunities through the pipeline.

**Key Takeaway :** Successful account-based marketing requires multi-threaded engagement. Companies should build relationships with multiple stakeholders inside target accounts rather than relying on a single contact to drive purchasing decisions.
