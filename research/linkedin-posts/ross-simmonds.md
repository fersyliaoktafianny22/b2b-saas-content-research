**Title :** YouTube as an Underrated Growth Channel for B2B SaaS

**Link :** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/rosssimmonds_youtube-videomarketing-aeo-activity-7472653370965798912-THDN?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAETE9j4BLR05wW6Rp6usGnUWg-kWo5O5mFE

**Date :** 17 June 2026

**Summary :** Ross argues that YouTube is one of the most underutilized growth opportunities for B2B SaaS companies. Based on Foundation Marketing's analysis of more than 8,000 B2B SaaS keywords, YouTube frequently appears in Google's top search results, especially for high-intent searches such as product demos, reviews, comparisons, and "best software" queries.
He recommends that SaaS companies focus on two initiatives: optimizing existing YouTube videos and creating new videos targeting high-priority keywords. 
According to Ross, success comes from aligning video content with user intent rather than producing flashy content.

**Key Takeaway :** B2B SaaS companies can gain significant organic visibility by treating YouTube as a search channel and optimizing video content around high-intent buyer keywords.
