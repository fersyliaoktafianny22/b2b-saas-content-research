## Post LinkedIn
**Title :** Everything in Marketing Works. Strategy Matters More Than Channels

**Link :** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/davegerhardt_spoiler-alert-everything-in-marketing-works-activity-7472966743162945537-oz8X?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAETE9j4BLR05wW6Rp6usGnUWg-kWo5O5mFE

**Date :** 18 June 2026

**Summary :** Dave argues that virtually every marketing channel can be effective, including YouTube, LinkedIn, webinars, events, email, partnerships, PR, and even traditional approaches. The real challenge is not choosing the "perfect" channel but understanding 
which channels fit a company's resources, strengths, timing, and goals. He emphasizes that many teams spread themselves too thin by trying to be everywhere at once. Instead, marketers should focus deeply on one channel, build momentum, and then expand into others. 
Success comes from having a clear strategy, strong positioning, and conviction rather than blindly following best practices.

**Key Takeaway :** Marketing success depends more on strategic focus and execution than on selecting the latest channel or tactic. Companies should concentrate on a few channels they can execute exceptionally well rather than trying to do everything at once.
