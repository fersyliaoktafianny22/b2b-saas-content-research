## Post LinkedIn 1
**Title :** Good prompt tracking starts with sample design.

**Link :** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/kevinindig_good-prompt-tracking-starts-with-sample-design-activity-7470197035565002752-T8ow?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAETE9j4BLR05wW6Rp6usGnUWg-kWo5O5mFE

**Date :** 13 June 2026

**Summary :** Kevin explains that effective prompt tracking should be treated as a structured research process rather than a simple ranking dashboard. For a B2B SaaS category such as CRM, 
he recommends building a representative sample of prompts across brand, category, and problem-intent searches. 
Each AI platform (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews) should be measured independently because their behaviors differ significantly. 
He also emphasizes the importance of evaluating results across multiple buyer personas and tracking metrics such as mention rate, citation rate, average position, sentiment, and associated brand attributes.

**Key Takeaway :** AI visibility measurement requires systematic sampling, persona-based analysis, and platform-specific tracking rather than relying on a single aggregated ranking metric.

## Post LinkedIn 2
**Title :** AI Citations and Brand Authority.

**Link :** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/kevinindig_two-brands-can-run-identical-on-page-optimization-activity-7473336241837731841-Dl3f?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAETE9j4BLR05wW6Rp6usGnUWg-kWo5O5mFE

**Date :** 18 June 2026

**Summary :** Kevin argues that AI citation visibility is influenced not only by on-page SEO optimization but also by off-property reputation. 
According to him, AI systems tend to rely on trusted sources and previously established authority signals when generating responses.
He explains that owned content such as company blogs contributes to authority, but third-party mentions from publications, analysts, partners, 
and competitors often carry more weight. As a result, brands with stronger external validation are more likely to receive AI citations even when on-page optimization is similar.

**Key Takeaway :** Winning AI citations requires building authority beyond your own website through credible third-party mentions and industry recognition.
